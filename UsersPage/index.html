Study helper is an innovative educational model that was designed to develop skills needed for the achieving best results in educational process.

What is Study Helper for a student?

Mobility

Study helper offers easy access to digital editions of books to enrich and enhance your learning experience. The platform can be used online or offline on multiple devices, anytime and anywhere.

Flexible curriculum

A student who likes studying remotely can independently decide when and how much time during the semester should devote to the study of the materials. Also student can build an individual training schedule up to their personal time.

Fast feedback

Communication with Tutors is carried out in different ways: both online and off-line. Consulting a tutor via e-mail is sometimes more efficient and faster than making a personal appointment with a professor.
